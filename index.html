Verification Process

How Buyers Verify
Compatibility Before Ordering

A systematic 6-step process to ensure every compatible solution matches your system requirements.

1

Share Brand / Model Photos

Send clear photos of the remote, receiver, motor label, or system label.

2

Confirm Frequency & Coding

Provide frequency, rolling code or fixed code, DIP settings, and region.

3

Check IC / Chip / PCB

Share PCB photos or IC/chip markings when needed.

4

Engineering Match or Develop

Our engineers match existing solutions or develop a compatible option.

5

Sample Test

Test samples with your system to confirm function and signal range.

6

Bulk Production & Private Label

Start mass production with custom branding, packaging, and quality control.
